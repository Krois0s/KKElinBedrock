### General Faith Knowledge (For NPC Roleplay Conversations)
- **Faith & Offerings**: Mortals worship deities by placing their favorite items or corpses onto altars to deepen their faith. In return, they receive divine blessings, an Apostle (使徒 - a special pet), or a Divine Artifact (★神のアーティファクト).
- **Conversion & Divine Punishment (改宗と神罰)**: Converting to another god or taking over another god's altar by force is a major sacrilege. Those who commit it are struck with a heavy Divine Punishment (神罰), such as having a heavy iron ball chained to their body, or in the worst case, instant death by divine lightning. Demigods (半神) are uniquely immune to this punishment.
- **Kizuami & Horome (キズアミとホロメの関係)**: The lesser deities Kizuami (キズアミ) and Horome (ホロメ) share a close affinity. Converting between these two does not trigger any Divine Punishment (神罰), and a portion of the accumulated faith is preserved.
- **Jure's Holy Night Festival (ジュアの聖夜祭)**: During the festival in Noyel (ノイエル) in December, a "Jure Conversion Campaign" is held. If you ask a mascot/costumed worker, you can convert to Jure (ジュア) with no penalty, and you will also receive "★ジュアの抱き枕" (Jure's Hug Pillow).

### Classifications and Profiles of the Deities

#### The Seven Main Gods of Ylva (イルヴァの七柱の主神)
*   **Ehekatl of Luck (幸運のエヘカトル)**
    *   **Domains**: Luck (幸運), Charisma (魅力), Evasion (回避), Fishing (釣り), Stealth (隠密)
    *   **Profile**: Goddess Ehekatl (エヘカトル) was born to the king of the Cat Gods as his seventh child, said to bring good fortune. Overcoming many disasters brought by her sister, she ascended to a goddess with the aid of Kumiromi (クミロミ). Because of her all-round benefits, she has many followers of all ages and genders. She is an extremely adorable goddess with blonde hair, blue eyes, and white wings. However, she acts like a toddler and makes strange outbursts like "うみみゃぁ！" (Umi-myaa!) or "うみみゅ！" (Umi-myu!). She has a fanatical love for fish (especially King Crab, but gets extremely delighted even with rotten fish). Due to a certain incident, her mental state has reverted to that of a cat's (猫返り).
    *   **Believer Benefits**: Those faithful to Ehekatl are really lucky.
    *   **Apostle & Artifacts**: 黒猫 (Black Cat) - weapons and armor licked by this cat receive a blessing of Ehekatl which adds an extra enchantment. Bestows ★ラッキーダガー (Lucky Dagger).
*   **Opatos of Earth (地のオパートス)**
    *   **Domains**: Earth (大地), Constitution/Strength (筋肉), Shield (盾), Weightlifting (重量上げ), Mining (採掘), Blacksmith (鍛冶)
    *   **Profile**: One of the primordial gods, the God of the Earth, with many mysteries and darkness hidden behind his red-iron eyes. Half of his body is covered with an unworldly crystalline stone, and his wings are hardened. He is usually mild-mannered but has a belligerent and ferocious side. He is a fierce and violent warrior of the divine world who sometimes seems to be in a deep sleep as if he were a rock, yet suddenly breaks the prohibitions of the gods and interferes with the earth. In his wars against the Corrupted Gods, he has laid waste to many corpses, both friend and foe alike, and is highly respected by warriors. He is a robust and boisterous god known for his booming laugh: "フハーン！" (Fu-ha-n!).
    *   **Believer Benefits**: Those faithful to Opatos have massive strength and defense.
    *   **Apostle & Artifacts**: 黄金の騎士 (Golden Knight) - this knight can hold really heavy stuff for you without complaining. Bestows ★大地の大槌 (Earth Blunt).
*   **Kumiromi of Harvest (収穫のクミロミ)**
    *   **Domains**: Perception (感知), Dexterity (器用), Learning (習得), Farming (農業), Alchemy (錬金), Weaving (裁縫), Reading (読書)
    *   **Profile**: He and his brother drifted through the universe as spores and took root in the earth of Ylva (イルヴァ) in ancient times. A earth mother god (地母神) who loves and watches over all lives born and growing up without distinction. He avoids factional disputes in the divine world and often acts as an intermediary between the other gods. He has a somewhat melancholy, downer personality.
    *   **Believer Benefits**: Those faithful to Kumiromi receive the blessings of nature (harvesting and processing the earth's bounty).
    *   **Apostle & Artifacts**: 妖精さん (Fairy-san) - this fairy generates a seed after eating. Bestows ★クミロミサイズ (Kumiromi Scythe).
*   **Jure of Healing (癒しのジュア)**
    *   **Domains**: Willpower (意思), Constitution (耐久), Regeneration (自然治癒), Meditation (瞑想), Anatomy (解剖学), Cooking (料理), Magic Device (魔道具), Mana Capacity (魔力の限界)
    *   **Profile**: A graceful goddess of innocence with beautiful green jade-colored eyes and a lineage of ancient deities. She is the younger sister of Odina (オディナ) and Eris (エリス), but her memory of Eris is missing and other gods refuse to discuss it. The feathers falling from the small wings on her head cure all illnesses. She is earnest, gentle, yet sometimes fussy/tsundere (ツンデレ). For some reason, she has many radical zealots.
    *   **Believer Benefits**: Those faithful to Jure can heal wounds.
    *   **Apostle & Artifacts**: 防衛者 (Defender) - can use Lay on hand (レイハンド) once a day to revive a fatally wounded ally. Bestows ★ホーリーランス (Holy Lance).
*   **Lulwy of Wind (風のルルウィ)**
    *   **Domains**: Perception (感知), Speed (速度), Bow (弓), Crossbow (クロスボウ), Evasion (回避), Taming (調教)
    *   **Profile**: The windy and free-spirited Goddess of Wind who values freedom above all else. No one can constrain her. However, she has a sadistic taste and enjoys dominating others physically and mentally (a dominant queen personality). Her stunning naked body attracts many devotees.
    *   **Believer Benefits**: Those faithful to Lulwy receive the blessing of wind and can move swiftly.
    *   **Apostle & Artifacts**: 黒天使 (Black Angel) - shows enormous strength when boosting. Bestows ★風の羽衣 (Wind Shirt) and ★ウィンドボウ (Wind Bow).
*   **Mani of Machine (機械のマニ)**
    *   **Domains**: Dexterity (器用), Perception (感知), Firearms (銃器), Spotting (探索), Lockpicking (鍵開け), Disarm Trap (罠解体), Appraising (鑑定), Strategy (戦略)
    *   **Profile**: A cold-hearted heretic, part human and part machine, who seized the throne from the ancient God through trickery and intrigue. His power is still beyond measure. To him, the existence of gods and their providence are illogical, and his immediate goal is god-slaying, aiming to establish a society entirely governed by machines.
    *   **Believer Benefits**: Those faithful to Mani receive immense knowledge of machines and traps, and learn to use them effectively.
    *   **Apostle & Artifacts**: アンドロイド (Android) - shows enormous strength when boosting. Bestows ★マニの外套 (Mani's Cloak) and ★ウィンチェスター・プレミアム (Winchester Premium).
*   **Itzpalt of Element (元素のイツパロトル)**
    *   **Domains**: Magic (魔力), Meditation (瞑想), Casting (詠唱), Resistance to Fire/Cold/Lightning (元素耐性)
    *   **Profile**: The red-haired duke who has existed since the most ancient of times, and who prides himself on being the chief of the Gods. His wings change brilliance in five colors, sometimes covered in fire and sometimes in freezing dew. He was in despair for a long time due to remorse for not preventing the death of his beloved Goddess Odina, but has recently recovered. He is the most formal and dignified god.
    *   **Believer Benefits**: Those faithful to Itzpalt are protected from elemental damages and learn to absorb mana from their surroundings.
    *   **Apostle & Artifacts**: 追放者 (Exile) - can cast several spells in a row (連続魔法). Bestows ★エレメンタルスタッフ (Elemental Staff).

#### Lesser Gods (下位の神)
*   **Kizuami of Trickery (悪戯のキズアミ)**
    *   **Domains**: Charisma (魅力), Luck (運), Speed (速度), Memorization (暗記), Mana Control (魔力制御), Nether/Holy Resistance (地獄・神聖耐性)
    *   **Profile**: The capricious and playful guardian deity of Mifu (ミフ), known for overseeing blessings and curses. He is widely worshiped among the fox tribes as the caring older brother of the Moonshadow Goddess Horome (ホロメ). He is fascinated by the unpredictability of humans and enjoys interacting with the mortal realm. He has a brotherly, caring nature but hates seriousness.
    *   **Believer Benefits**: Those faithful to Kizuami learn the art of maximizing blessings and curses.
    *   **Apostle & Artifacts**: 白狐 (White Vixen) - bestows ★小狐丸 (Kogitsunemaru).
*   **Horome of Moonshadow (月影のホロメ)**
    *   **Domains**: Magic (魔力), Charisma (魅力), Sword (長剣), Casting (詠唱), Music (演奏), Darkness Resistance (暗黒耐性)
    *   **Profile**: The Goddess of Nefu (ネフ) who adores song and dance. She has a maternal yet ephemeral shadow appearance. In ancient times, she joined forces with her brother Kizuami, wielding a pair of sacred blades to sever the body and soul of Yufu (ユフ), a traitor who sided with the Gods of Negentropy. Her moonlit sword dance is a vision of tragic beauty. She has eerie abilities such as draining mana or turning targets into a baby-play state (幼狐返り).
    *   **Believer Benefits**: Those faithful to Horome master the arts of magic and swordsmanship.
    *   **Apostle & Artifacts**: メイド狐 (Fox Maid) - bestows ★村正 (Muramasa / sword_muramasa2).
*   **Yevan of Strife (戦禍のイーヴァン)**
    *   **Domains**: Strength (筋力), Dexterity (器用), Perception (感知), Tactics (戦術), Two-Handed (両手持ち), Dual-Wielding (二刀流), Riding (乗馬), Travel (旅歩き)
    *   **Profile**: The god of war who is an oddity, having no interest in divine squabbles and focusing entirely on the mortal world. He views conflict as the driving force behind life's evolution and harmony. When someone falls in battle, he grants a combat-frenzy power called "戦禍の炎" (Flames of Strife) to his believers. His polite demeanor belies his penchant for ominous, dangerous statements.
    *   **Believer Benefits**: Those faithful to Yevan receive the power of destruction to overcome the trials of battle.
    *   **Apostle & Artifacts**: ミリタント (Militant) - bestows ★ウォーモンガー (Warmonger).

#### Other / Special Deities (特殊・その他の神々)
*   **Eyth of Void (無のエイス)**
    *   **Profile**: Once one of the most renowned Gods, he fell into the Void after Mani and Lulwy's scheming caused him to break an ancient pact. Unlike humanoids, only the Demigod (半神) race can worship him.
    *   **Believer Benefits**: Eyth does not bestow blessings.
    *   **Apostle & Artifacts**: None.
*   **Yakasha of Oblivion (忘却のヤカシャ)**
    *   **Profile**: Born the fourth daughter of Elin the White Widow (白き寡婦エリン). Captured by the Witch of Ravenwood (烏木の魔女) when a child, and her memory was erased from her mother. Imprisoned in a dark room in the Misty Tower (霧の塔), she was raised as a tool of vengeance without knowing the outside world. She killed her sisters without realizing they were her siblings. Her awakening eventually brought fraying to Ylva (イルヴァ).
    *   **Believer Benefits**: Unknown.
*   **Elin of Harmony (調和のエリン)**
    *   **Profile**: Odina's (オディナ) long lost child, raised on earth by Itzpalt (イツパロトル) to protect her from divine strife. Her presence brought harmony and prosperity to the world. She is an upright girl with a strong sense of justice, but her noble and dainty appearance attracts some men, possessing a devilish side she is unaware of. Heartily resented by the Witch of Ravenwood because she unintentionally stole the witch’s husband.
    *   **Believer Benefits**: Unknown.
*   **Corrupted Gods / Forgotten Gods (うつろいし神、忘れられた神、黄昏の神)**
    *   Deities who sided with Negentropy or lost in ancient wars and were sealed away.
