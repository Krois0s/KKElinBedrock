### Chronological History of Ylva (イルヴァの歴史)
In "イルヴァ", the concept of "Era (紀)" refers to cycle of rise and fall of civilizations, typically lasting tens of thousands of years (though some are very short).

#### 1st Era: Age of Great Void (一紀: 欠落の時代)
- **Overview**: The birth of Ylva. This era is completely shrouded in mystery, and its duration remains unknown.

#### 2nd Era: Age of Beginning (二紀: はじまりの時代)
- **Overview**: The age of the first mortals and the primordial Gods. Itzpalt (イツパロトル) was the first to perceive Ylva, followed by Opatos (オパートス) and other deities (including the Gods of Chaos). Many living creatures were born. Relationships between gods and mortals were stable, and deities kept their rivalries in check.
- **Key Events**:
  - Appearance of Itzpalt (イツパロトル)
  - Appearance of Opatos (オパートス)
  - Appearance of other deities and Chaos Gods
  - End of the era triggered by a massive divine war (神々の戦争)

#### 3rd Era: Age of Experiment (三紀: 実験の時代)
- **Overview**: Gods treated Ylva as a massive laboratory, carrying out rampant experiments. They engineered new races as vanguard soldiers, modified ecosystems, split continents, and destroyed the sister moon Lacrina (ラクリナ). Driven by anger, the various races of Ylva—centered around the Apostles (使徒) of the gods—rebelled against their creators and exiled them. The Gods of Negentropy (負の神) caused the worst havoc during this era.
- **Key Events**:
  - Splitting of the continents (大陸の分断)
  - Appearance of Lulwy (ルルウィ)
  - Destruction of the sister moon Lacrina (姉妹月ラクリナの破壊)
  - Rebellion by the Apostles (使徒達の反乱)
  - Exile of the Gods (神々の追放)

#### 4th Era: Age of Loss (四紀: 喪失の時代)
- **Overview**: An era plagued by natural disasters and conflicts due to the severe scars of the gods' experiments. Characterized by inter-species crossbreeding, loss of racial purpose, plagues, and mass suicides born of madness and despair. Humanoid races (人間系種族) were still a minority. At the end of the era, the returning gods wiped out (一掃) all life on Ylva as both revenge and reflection, establishing minimum rules to prevent recurrence. This laid the foundation for the precursor of the Eternal Pact (永遠の盟約).
- **Key Events**:
  - Massive natural disasters (大自然災害)
  - Plagues (疫病の流行)
  - Return of the Gods (神々の再来)
  - Wiping out of all living creatures (生物を一掃)
  - Birth of the draft of the Eternal Pact (永遠の盟約のひながたの誕生)

#### 5th Era: Age of Harmony (五紀: 調和の時代)
- **Overview**: Under the quiet watch of the gods, diverse civilizations and species flourished. Gods possessed fantasy-like, non-humanoid appearances. Mortals developed advanced, high-level science and eventually traveled to the moon. However, another massive divine war broke out at the end of the era, shifting the leading role back to the gods.
- **Key Events**:
  - Appearance of Kumiromi (クミロミ)
  - Outbreak of a full-scale war among the Gods (神々の間の本格的な戦争開始)

#### 6th Era: Age of War (六紀: 闘争の時代)
- **Overview**: While main gods fought, the Gods of Negentropy (負の神) plotted to retake Ylva and grew too powerful for single deities to challenge. To counter this, other gods united and signed the true Eternal Pact (真・永遠の盟約). Although they successfully exiled the negative gods, the main gods suffered heavy losses. To heal themselves, they wiped out (一掃) all life on Ylva and withdrew to their respective domains. Only Eyth (エイス), who overslept, was left behind. Since then, gods rarely intervene directly.
- **Key Events**:
  - Rise of the Gods of Negentropy (負の神々の台頭)
  - Appearance of Jure (ジュア)
  - Signing of the True Eternal Pact (真・永遠の盟約の締結)
  - Wiping out of all living creatures (生物を一掃)
  - Exile and departure of all gods except Eyth (エイス以外の神々の追放、離去)

#### 7th Era: Age of Enlightenment "Eyth-Teir" (七紀: 光明の時代「エイス・テール」)
- **Overview**: A materialistic era closest to the modern human world, driven by humans. Magic did not exist. Instead, mechanical engineering and difference engines (階差機関) allowed humans to expand into space. In the late period, resource exhaustion and environmental pollution grew. Naturalists seeking hermit-like lives increased, mockingly called Elves (エルフ) or Hermits (ハーミット).
- **Key Events**:
  - Creation of the secret organization Zeiren (ゼイレン)
  - Increase of naturalists (自然回帰主義者たちの増加)
  - Natural collapse of the civilization (自然消滅)

#### 8th Era: Age of Descension (八紀: 凋落の時代)
- **Overview**: Material civilization collapsed. The land was polluted and devastated, leaving ruins and vast deserts. Surviving humans continued to decline.

#### 9th Era: Age of Reminiscence (九紀: 追憶の時代)
- **Overview**: Self-operating machines from the previous era were buried in sand and fell into eternal sleep. The last of humanity silently died out. White sand continued to fall, covering the ground. The planet fell into total silence. Eventually, wandering gods quietly returned (神々の静かな帰還) to Ylva.
- **Key Events**:
  - Quiet return of the Gods (神々の静かな帰還)

#### 10th Era: Age of Calamity "Rem-Id" (十紀: 災厄の時代「レム・イド」)
- **Overview**: Rejecting the materialism of Eyth-Teir (エイス・テール), this era saw highly developed biochemistry and created a utopia without wealth inequality. However, the appearance of unstable individuals called Yutas (ユタス), combined with the discovery of Ether (エーテル), which Yutas monopolized, disrupted the peace. A biological experiment birthed the bacteria Meshera (メシェーラ), which went out of control, causing a world-ending calamity. Ended with the Miracle of Vindale (ヴィンデールの奇跡).
- **Key Events**:
  - Emergence of the Yutas (ユタス) problem
  - Discovery of Ether (エーテル)
  - Birth of Meshera (メシェーラ)
  - Meshera's outbreak and world collapse (メシェーラの暴走)
  - Miracle of Vindale (ヴィンデールの奇跡)

#### 11th Era: Age of Revival "Sierra-Teir" (十一紀: 再生の時代「シエラ・テール」)
- **Overview**: Civilization restarted from a few survivors of the Miracle of Vindale (ヴィンデールの奇跡). Magic, lost since the 6th Era, was rediscovered, and alchemy flourished. Ancient relics of Eyth-Teir (エイス・テール) were treated as taboo. The seven main deities established a truce (神々の休戦協定) to avoid over-interfering. Humans adopted the Sierra New Calendar (シエラ新暦). The world has become a mixture of great magic and crude machinery. God Mani (マニ) appeared. Elona's protagonist awakens in the summer of Year 517 of the Sierra New Calendar (シエラ新暦517年).
- **Key Events**:
  - Migration from Vindale (ヴィンデール) to the outside world
  - Mortals adapt physically to survive outside
  - Founding of Ancient Euderna (古代エウダーナの建国)
  - Revival of the Gods (神々の復興)
  - Founding of Palmia (パルミア建国)
  - Precursor state of Zanan (ザナン) established
  - Creation of Hillfolk (丘の民)
  - Interstitial Era of Oblivion "Nark-Domara" (忘却の時代「ナーク・ドマーラ」)
  - Appearance of Mani (マニ)

### Sierra New Calendar Timeline (シエラ新暦・簡易年表)
- **Year 0**: Truce of the Gods (神々の休戦協定).
- **Year 8**: Birth of Lumiest (ルミエストの地誕生).
- **Year 47**: Monfelinibel (モンフェルニベール) founded.
- **Year 108**: Verissia (ヴェーリッシア) founded.
- **Year 155**: Zanan (ザナン) rises from its predecessor state.
- **Year 174–176**: Zanan-Euderna War (ザナン・エウダーナ戦争) - Euderna wins; Zanan becomes a satellite state.
- **Year 229**: Euderna occupies Kikkas (キッカス).
- **Year 391**: Start of the Age of Sail. Verissia prospers.
- **Year 404**: Yerles (イエル) founded.
- **Year 411–412**: First Euderna-Yerles War (第一次エウダーナ・イエル戦争) - Yerles repels Euderna; Kikkas gains independence from Euderna.
- **Year 415**: Yerles immigrants flow into Kikkas and Nisa (ニーサ).
- **Year 420–424**: Second Euderna-Yerles War (第二次エウダーナ・イエル戦争) - Euderna wins. Euderna massacres Yerles immigrants in Kikkas and Nisa. Age of Sail ends.
- **Year 455–458**: Third Euderna-Yerles War (第三次エウダーナ・イエル戦争) - Yerles defeats Euderna. (Year 455, March 21: Jabi's birthday).
